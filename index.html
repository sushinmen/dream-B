Core technology: DreamEncode™

Patent-backed pipeline (e.g. KR101044096B1) built to turn neural patterns into usable text—not a toy demo, but a validated stack.

  1. REM detection Brain and body signals analyzed to lock onto REM windows with precision.
  2. DreamEncode™ engine Waveform data streamed into natural-language mapping; high-accuracy pattern recognition on the path from signal to words.
  3. Alpha & theta Creative-thinking and dream-related bands separated and analyzed—not one blob of “brain noise.”
  4. Real-time write As soon as something is detected, it’s written to onboard USB storage—no waiting until morning.
  5. Trust & IP Core methods tied to granted patent family—designed for reliability, not vibes-only marketing.

In the app: waveform visualization and a dashboard over time—see the nights stack up, not just a single cryptic line.

Pull boundless night ideas into the day

DreamEncode™ separation keeps alpha (roughly 8–12Hz) creative cues and theta (roughly 4–8Hz) dream-language channels distinct, so what you read back matches what your night actually looked like on the sensor side—not one mashed-up guess.
